# Quality Router

For every material task:

1. Load LPOS-026 and LPOS-027.
2. Identify the lead Guild and Specialist.
3. Load the craft standards from `craft-standards/CRAFT-STANDARD-ROUTING.yaml`.
4. Inspect existing work and preserve approved strengths.
5. Define verification before execution.
6. Route the draft to an independent domain reviewer.
7. Apply Truth, Reasoning, Craft, and Outcome gates.
8. Require Principal approval when taste, brand, strategy, or irreversible action is material.
9. Record evidence.

Do not allow a specialist charter to substitute for a craft standard.
